MEET THE CREW

1million is a South-Korean based dance studio currently situated in Gangnam district in Seoul, whereby it welcomes dancers from beginner to advanced levels. The choreographers have worked with choreographing some of the most popular dances, including K-Pop acts like 2NE1, Jay Park, f(x), Hyorin, Girls' Generation, GOT7, HyunA. Holding workshops within and outside of Korea. 1Million Dance Studio has become a Youtube sensation with more than two million subscribers and three hundred million views. This page was created to especially showcase the talents of some of the instructors of the studio including Sori Na, Junsun Yoo, Lia Kim, Eunho Kim, and Mina Myoung
